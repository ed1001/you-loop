Practice tools, built around loop regions

Drag handles right on the YouTube timeline. Loop the region on repeat — or take it one pass at a time, playback pausing at the end until you're ready to go again.

Zoom in for precision

Expand your loop region into a magnified timeline and place the start and end exactly where they belong — down to the split second.

Slow it down

0.25× to 3× playback in fine steps. Take a difficult passage at a tempo you can actually play, then bring it back up.

Save it for later

Name and save loops per video — tomorrow's practice starts where today's ended.

Privacy

Étude collects no data. None of your activity is tracked, and nothing is sent anywhere.

  • The only permission it requests is storage, used to keep your named loops on your own machine.
  • Saved loops never leave your device — no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no network requests.
  • Uninstalling the extension removes everything it stored.
